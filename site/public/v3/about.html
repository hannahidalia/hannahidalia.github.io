From Burnout to Brilliance

My journey from corporate exhaustion to helping professionals rediscover their spark and build sustainable success.

My Story

For years, I was the person who had it all together. A rising star in a demanding consulting career, I was the one people came to for advice. But behind the success, I was drowning in a sea of 60-hour work weeks, constant pressure, and a growing sense of emptiness.

I was a classic case of high-functioning burnout. I didn't know it then, of course. I just thought that the exhaustion, the cynicism, and the feeling of being disconnected from my own life was the price of success.

My breaking point came on the day I received a major promotion. Instead of joy, I felt a profound sense of dread. That evening, I knew I had to make a change. I had to find a way to be successful without sacrificing myself.

My path to recovery was a journey of rediscovery. It involved a lot of unlearning, a lot of boundary-setting, and a lot of getting honest with myself about what I truly wanted. It wasn't easy, but it was worth it. I learned that it's possible to have a thriving career that energizes you, not depletes you.

Now, as a certified coach, I help other professionals navigate their own journey from burnout to brilliance. I've been in the trenches, and I know the way out. My mission is to help you build a career and a life that you love.

My Philosophy & Approach

Beyond 'Hustle Culture'

I believe that true success isn't about grinding harder, but about working smarter. It's about creating a life where your career is a source of energy, not a drain on it. My approach is to help you build a sustainable career that aligns with your values and passions.

A Holistic Approach

Burnout isn't just a work problem; it's a life problem. That's why I take a holistic approach to coaching, addressing everything from your mindset and energy levels to your relationships and work-life balance. We'll work together to create a life that's not just successful, but also joyful and fulfilling.

Your Unique Path to Success

There's no one-size-fits-all solution to burnout. That's why I work with you to create a personalized coaching plan that's tailored to your unique needs, goals, and circumstances. I'll help you identify the root causes of your burnout and develop a strategy for lasting change.

From Surviving to Thriving

My ultimate goal is to help you move from a state of survival to a state of thriving. I want to help you build a life that you're excited to wake up to every morning. A life where you're not just going through the motions, but actively creating a career and a life that you love.
