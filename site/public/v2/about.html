From High-Stakes Boardrooms to Embodied Wisdom

Like many of my clients, my career began in the fast-paced world of corporate strategy. I thrived on the pressure, the intellectual challenge, and the tangible wins. From the outside, I was the picture of success. On the inside, I was running on empty, disconnected from myself and the work that once brought me joy.

My turning point wasn't a single event, but a slow, creeping realization that the relentless pursuit of 'more' was leading me further away from the impact and fulfillment I truly craved. I knew there had to be a different way to lead—one that didn't demand the sacrifice of my own well-being.

"I discovered that the key to sustainable leadership wasn't in a new strategy, but in a new state of being."

This realization sparked a deep dive into the worlds of somatic psychology, biodynamic craniosacral therapy, and psycho-spiritual development. I wasn't just learning new techniques; I was fundamentally rewiring my own nervous system and rediscovering a source of wisdom within me that was far more powerful than any external validation. I learned to lead not from my head, but from a place of deep, embodied knowing.

My mission now is to guide other women in high-stakes roles back to themselves. I provide the tools and the dedicated support to help you move from burnout to brilliance, from self-doubt to self-trust, and to lead with the authentic, unshakeable presence that is your birthright.
